The Door Of Hope School

Story of our School and we stand for this Initiative?

Collaborate With Legacy Of Christ Ministery

Let me tell you how we started the school and what we thought about opening this school,my siblings and I all studied in school and the people around us were very poor The financial conditions of our family were not so good but still our father gave us good education Our father's thought was that I should give my children a good education, Then we thought that these people around us do not have enough resources, so what can we do for them, so we took a start in which we invited children to our homes. Started giving free education.When we started, we had two or four children and they were not able to meet their expenses like stationery, copy book, pencil and many other things which were becoming very difficult to provide for them. Then my siblings and I all together started buying books and pencil sharpeners and other things for these children from our pocket money. And then I started talking to my friends that bro I am running a school which has many children who don't have resources to study. And I am very grateful to my friends, brothers and relatives who helped these children with their little money.Gradually we had more children and their expenses were increasing then I started collecting donations from people. And I am very grateful to those people who have helped me in starting the school,my family and I had a passion to impart that education to others just as we had received a good education. We expressed the school in 2018 and today it is 2023 and our school is running continuously. God knows the plans we have for our people and God provides us with resources as He does. I am very grateful to my God who never leaves me and is with me in every way
