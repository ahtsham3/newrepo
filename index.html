About The Door of Hope Welfare

Who are we?

We are followers of Jesus seeking to be an authentic expression of the kingdom of Jesus in the twenty-first century. We believe Jesus is the King of the world and as an expression of Jesus’ kingdom; we are following him on a journey of faith as a worshiping and just community.
We are people who worship God together, keeping our eyes on Jesus in our day-to-day lives. We are committed to justice, working for the good of all.

    We are group of people intentionally living life together. we are here to serve humannity to all people and we enjoy to work with them.
